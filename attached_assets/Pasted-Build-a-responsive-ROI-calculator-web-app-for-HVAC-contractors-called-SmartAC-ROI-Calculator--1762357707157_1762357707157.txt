Build a responsive ROI calculator web app for HVAC contractors called "SmartAC ROI Calculator."

### PURPOSE
Show contractors how much profit growth, truck-roll savings, and membership revenue improvement they could achieve by adopting SmartAC’s platform. The calculator compares "Before SmartAC" vs. "With SmartAC" performance over one year and five years.

### DESIGN & BRANDING
- Use ServiceTitan Hacks / SmartAC color palette: red (#ED254E), dark gray (#333333), white background accents, light gray cards.
- Modern clean layout with clear data cards and large numeric outputs.
- Responsive for desktop and mobile.
- Include SmartAC logo placeholder at top and a “Calculate ROI” button in SmartAC red.
- Results section should appear only after calculation.

### INPUT FIELDS (user editable)
Label each clearly with tooltips showing example defaults:
1. Active Memberships (default 5000)
2. Annual Membership Cost ($/yr) (default 240)
3. Membership Close Rate (%) (default 20)
4. Annual Retention Rate (%) (default 70)
5. Average Truck Rolls per Member per Year (default 2)
6. Average Truck Roll Cost ($) (default 400)
7. Average Revenue per Member per Year ($) (default 1000)
8. Gross Margin (%) (default 50)

### SMARTAC IMPROVEMENT FACTORS (pre-set sliders or constants)
- Conversion Improvement: 20% → 40%
- Retention Improvement: 70% → 90%
- Truck Rolls per Member: 2 → 1
- Virtual Visit Savings: $300 per home per year
- SmartAC Platform Cost: $1000/month ($12,000/year)

### CALCULATIONS
Use the following formulas:
- Before Revenue = (Active Memberships × Avg Revenue per Member)
- Before Profit = Before Revenue × (Gross Margin / 100)
- Before Truck Roll Cost = (Truck Rolls × Roll Cost × Active Memberships)
- After Revenue = (Active Memberships × (CloseRate_after / CloseRate_before) × Avg Revenue per Member)
- After Profit = After Revenue × (Gross Margin / 100)
- After Truck Roll Cost = (Truck Rolls_after × Roll Cost × Active Memberships)
- Annual Savings = (Before Truck Roll Cost − After Truck Roll Cost) + (After Profit − Before Profit)
- 5-Year Compounding Growth = After Revenue × 1.279 (for base) vs. 2.79× multiplier (for SmartAC scenario)
- ROI (%) = ((Annual Savings − 12000) / 12000) × 100

### OUTPUT SECTION
Display the following dynamically:
- Membership Revenue Before / After
- Profit Before / After
- Truck-Roll Cost Before / After
- Annual Savings
- 5-Year Growth Projection (bar chart)
- ROI (%)

### UI ELEMENTS
- Use cards for “Inputs,” “Results,” and “Assumptions.”
- Include animated number counters on calculate.
- Use a bar chart or line chart comparing Before vs. After over 5 years.
- Add CTA at bottom: “Schedule a SmartAC Demo” (link placeholder).

### BEHAVIOR
- All calculations run instantly client-side when “Calculate ROI” is pressed.
- Reset button clears all fields.
- Tooltips on every input explaining industry averages.

### COPY
Header: “See Your 5-Year Profit Growth with SmartAC”
Sub-header: “Enter a few numbers to see how SmartAC turns memberships into a growth engine.”
