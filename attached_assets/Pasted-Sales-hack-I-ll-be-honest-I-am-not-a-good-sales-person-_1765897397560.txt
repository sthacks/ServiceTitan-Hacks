The only reason I’m getting better is because I learned from my mistakes which before AI was not possible to do here’s how I do it for about 25 bucks a month

The only way to know is the only way to get better is to know exactly what happened. The best football players watch tape the best golfers analyze their swing. The best baseball players analyze their swing and practice.

The problem is Sales is when you practice and you mess up you really don’t know exactly where you messed up

What I started to do during Covid was record all of my Zoom calls and use a note taker during the call

Initially, the note takers were great because it would put together a list of action items so I could focus on the call rather than try to write down notes

And I found that I’m just a horrible note taker in general 45 minute calls with week notes

When I started doing sales calls, I continue to record the conversations, but when ChatGPT came out, I realized that I could take those transcripts at the conversation conversations and analyze them deeper

So I would take 10 similar type calls, export the transcript to PDF upload all the PDFs into ChatGPT and ask it a prompt that said something like this. This is transcripts from the last 10 sales calls that I did. I want you to analyze them and tell me what’s the number one thing that I do wrong. Where does the conversation go wrong? What questions do customers ask that I don’t answer correctly and what could I have said instead?

I can even ask it do I talk too much do I talk too fast

Really the possibilities of the feedback you can get our endless

Today they have something called a Plaud note pin, which you can wear on your wrist and easily record in person conversations transcribe them and even within Plaud’s app have something that
Will give you this feedback
